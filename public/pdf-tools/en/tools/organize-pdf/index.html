About This Tool

Organize PDF provides an intuitive drag-and-drop interface for rearranging pages in your PDF documents. Reorder pages, duplicate important sections, or remove unwanted pages with ease.

The visual page thumbnails make it easy to identify content and arrange pages exactly as you need them. Perfect for restructuring documents, creating custom page orders, or cleaning up scanned files.

All organization happens locally in your browser, ensuring your documents remain private.

How to Use

  1. 1

    Upload Your PDF

    Drag and drop your PDF file or click to select the document you want to organize.

  2. 2

    Rearrange Pages

    Drag page thumbnails to reorder them. Click the duplicate or delete buttons on each page as needed.

  3. 3

    Save and Download

    Click Save to apply your changes and download the reorganized PDF.

Use Cases

Fix Page Order

Correct the order of pages that were scanned or combined incorrectly.

Create Custom Order

Arrange pages in a specific sequence for presentations or reports.

Remove Unwanted Pages

Delete blank pages, duplicates, or irrelevant content from documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I duplicate pages?

Yes, you can duplicate any page and place the copy anywhere in the document.

Is there an undo function?

Yes, you can undo and redo changes. You can also reset to the original order at any time.

Can I organize multiple PDFs together?

This tool works with one PDF at a time. To combine and organize multiple PDFs, first merge them using the Merge PDF tool.
